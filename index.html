About Me

Designing with purpose

Hi, I'm Rushkha — a UI/UX designer and final-year IT student who delivers design that is grounded in evidence, not assumptions. I research users, map their pain points, and translate insights into interfaces that are clean, accessible, and purposeful.

Currently in my final year of BA (Hons) in Information Technology at the University of Sri Jayewardenapura, I've focused my academic journey on understanding why people behave the way they do with digital products — and designing solutions that feel effortless because they're built on real insight.

From semi-structured interviews to SUS-scored usability tests, I own the full UX process — and I bring the same rigour to every project, regardless of scale.
